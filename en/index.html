Solfianza, your trusted partner for financing your projects

Solfianza supports individuals in realising their projects through simple, accessible and tailored consumer credit solutions. A subsidiary of Crédit Agricole Consumer Finance, the company draws on the expertise and financial strength of a leading banking group to offer reliable and competitive financing solutions.

Whether you are planning to purchase a vehicle, carry out renovations, organise a trip or any other personal project, Solfianza offers credit solutions designed to adapt to your situation and your goals. Through a fully digital process, your application is simplified, fast and secure, with a decision in principle obtained within minutes.

Reconnue pour la qualité de son accompagnement et son engagement envers ses clients, Solfianza place la transparence et la proximité au cœur de son approche. Your questions about consumer credit

  • Solfianza offers personal loans (auto, home works, leisure…), revolving credit with or without a card, as well as debt consolidation to group your loans into a single reduced monthly payment.

  • After electronically signing your contract, a legal 14-calendar-day withdrawal period applies. Once this period has elapsed, funds are generally transferred within 1 to 3 business days.

  • Go to our online simulator, choose your project and amount, then complete the application form. You will receive an immediate decision in principle and can finalise your application entirely online.

  • Generally, you will be asked for: a valid identity document, a recent proof of address, your latest payslips or proof of income, and your bank account details (IBAN).

  • There is no legal maximum age for taking out a consumer loan. Solfianza reviews each application based on the borrower's repayment capacity. However, borrower insurance policies may include specific age conditions.
